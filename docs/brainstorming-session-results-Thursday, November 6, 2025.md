### Session Goal

To generate creative and practical ideas for a university AI chatbot project focused on providing information about study programs.

### Key Project Principles

*   **Simplicity:** The application should be easy to make and easy to use.
*   **Focus:** The primary goal is to provide quick and easy access to information about study programs.
*   **General Advice:** The chatbot will provide general information rather than personalizing responses based on user roles.
*   **Single Source of Truth:** The chatbot will exclusively use the university's official website as its information source.
